Understanding critical factors of Minority and Transferred Students in the U.S. Schools.

Technologies used: Jupyter Notebook, Python(Altair, Numpy, Pandas, Seaborn)

Performed Simple Linear Regression and Multiple Linear Regression on college enrollment data to determine if any factors affect the number of college students, by calculating the goodness of fit using R-square and utilizing heat-map to detect multicollinearity between different factors.
