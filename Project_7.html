Dianping Business Scrape

Scraped Children’s Libraries information in China from DianPing.com (the Chinese equivalent of Yelp) with HTTP requests and sessions, extracted website cookies using selenium webdriver and bypassed Captcha with a modified request header. Retrieved a list of children’s libraries in over 2000 Chinese cities with the URL of their respective web pages. Parsed HTML code iteratively into BeautifulSoup object, located and scraped relevant information with XPath, and exported the final result into CSV files.
