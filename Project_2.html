Poverty Prediction with Census and Education Data

Technologies used:R (ggplot, ROCR, ggridges, dplyr, glmnet, tree, randomForest,denextend)

Perform PCA on county-level census data and calculate PVE for each principal component. Conduct hierarchical clustering with complete linkage on each principal component. Predict poverty rate with different classification methods such as decision tree, logistic regression, Lasso regression, random forest, LDA. Computed the AUC rate to measure the performance of each classification method.
